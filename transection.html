How to Find Your UTR Number

A UTR (Unique Transaction Reference) number is a 12-digit code that identifies your bank transaction. Here's how to find it:

1

PhonePe

  • Open the PhonePe app
  • Go to 'History' section
  • Select your payment to Fresh Atta
  • UTR will be shown as 'Transaction ID' or 'Reference Number'
2

Google Pay

  • Open Google Pay app
  • Tap on 'Payments' tab
  • Find your payment to Fresh Atta
  • UTR will be shown as 'UPI Transaction ID'
3

Paytm

  • Open Paytm app
  • Go to 'Passbook'
  • Select your payment transaction
  • UTR will be shown as 'Transaction ID'
4

Bank Apps

  • Open your bank's mobile app
  • Go to 'Transactions' or 'Statement'
  • Find your UPI payment
  • UTR will be in transaction details

Important Notes:

  • UTR number is always 12 digits long
  • It may take 1-2 minutes to appear after payment
  • If you can't find it, check your SMS for payment confirmation
  • For failed transactions, UTR will still be generated
